Principal, Founder

Jessica Corr

Jessica Corr is an award-winning industrial designer and an Associate Professor at Parsons School of Design. With a focus on hospitality, Jessica has consulted for Interior Designers such as tonychi, BAR Studio, Studio Razavi, Atelier Marsh, and Whynought. As cofounder of 44 she provides the nexus between beauty and innovation.

Principal, Founder

William Paley

William Paley is a multidisciplinary designer with a foundation in architecture, interior decoration, and fine arts. After 25 years of collaborating with tonychi, William cofounded 44 to seek beauty through deep personal partnerships.

Director of Strategy

Abbas Jamali

Abbas has diverse experience as a strategic consultant in product development, consumer goods, fashion and lifestyle industries. He is the creative consultant and strategic director for The Tehran Times, the first Iranian street style blog. He was also the Managing Director for Maison Araz Fazaeli, and was integral in launching the brand and introducing the collection to Paris Fashion Week. In his multidisciplinary experience, Abbas works across design fields, collaborating with creatives and business individuals to produce breakthrough brands.

Director of Visual Design

Gerald Morin

Gerald Morin is an award-winning graphic designer whose experience in building brands has given him expertise in a wide range of design disciplines.
